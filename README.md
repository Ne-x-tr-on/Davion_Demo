# Davion

**Davion** is a comprehensive platform for engineers, STEM students, and self-taught builders to turn ideas into real projects, document their journey, collaborate with others, and get recognized based on what they build.

**Live Platform:** https://davion.netlify.app

---

# What Davion Is

Davion is more than just a website. It is a full ecosystem for building, documenting, and proving engineering work. Unlike traditional resumes or certificates, Davion focuses on **real projects and actual skills demonstrated through work**.

---

# Core Concept

**Idea → Build → Document → Collaborate → Showcase → Get Opportunities**

A user can come to Davion with an idea, such as building a robot, and the platform guides them to:

- Find the most relevant and community-verified tutorials  
- Understand required components and their connections  
- Generate or access circuit diagrams  
- Track project progress through logs and milestones  
- Collaborate with team members  
- Build a portfolio automatically from completed projects  

---

# How Davion Works

## 1. User Account

- Sign up or log in  
- Create a profile with skills, interests, and experience  
- Track progress across all projects  

---

## 2. Dashboard

The dashboard provides:

- Ongoing projects  
- Recommended tutorials and projects  
- Active hackathons  
- Community activity  
- Marketplace updates  

---

## 3. Creating a Project

When starting a project, users input:

- Title and description  
- Tech stack and tools  
- Project type and difficulty  
- Tags (e.g., IoT, Robotics, AI)  
- Team members or collaborators  

---

## 4. Project Workspace

Each project has:

### Project Logs

- Record daily or weekly progress  
- Upload files, images, or schematics  
- Track milestones and completion status  

### Collaboration

- Invite team members  
- Assign roles (Manager, Engineer, Contributor)  
- Communicate through internal messages  

### Resources

- Recommended tutorials based on community rankings  
- Component list with quantities and prices  
- Circuit diagrams or templates for guidance  

---

## 5. Portfolio & Proof-of-Work

- Each completed project generates a portfolio entry  
- Includes project files, progress logs, and milestones  
- Verified skill badges are awarded based on completed work  
- Recruiters and mentors can view verified portfolios  

---

## 6. Hackathons

- Participate in local and pan-African competitions  
- Form teams automatically or manually  
- Mentors guide projects  
- Verified judges review work  
- Certificates and portfolio recognition provided  

---

## 7. Marketplace

- Sell engineering kits, tutorials, or services  
- Browse other builders’ offerings  
- Pricing and ratings visible for all products  

---

## 8. Community

- Discussion forums organized by topic (Robotics, IoT, Electronics, Software Engineering, Mechanical)  
- Ask questions, share knowledge, and get mentorship  
- Build a network of like-minded builders  

---

## 9. Settings & Features

Users can manage:

- Profile and skills  
- Privacy settings  
- Notifications for projects, hackathons, and marketplace activity  
- Collaboration permissions for each project  

---

## Who Davion Helps

- **Students & Learners:** Build portfolios and track skills before graduation  
- **Engineers & Professionals:** Showcase real projects, collaborate, and monetize skills  
- **Mentors:** Guide learners, approve logs, and moderate forums  
- **Recruiters & Companies:** Hire based on proof-of-work portfolios  
- **Hackathon Organizers:** Run competitions and manage teams  
- **Marketplace Sellers:** Sell kits, tutorials, and services to a targeted builder audience  

---

## Goal

Davion is designed to provide **everything a builder needs**, from starting a project to gaining recognition, collaborating with peers, and monetizing expertise. It bridges the gap between ideas and opportunities by focusing on **actual work and proven skills**.
